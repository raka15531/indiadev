Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How soon will you respond to my inquiry?

We respond to WhatsApp messages within 5 minutes during business hours. Emails are answered within 24 hours. Phone calls are answered immediately during business hours.

Do you offer free consultation?

Yes! We offer free 30-minute consultation sessions to understand your requirements and provide initial suggestions. You can schedule one via WhatsApp or phone.

What information do I need to provide for a quote?

Please provide: 1) Your business details, 2) Type of website needed, 3) Number of pages, 4) Special features required, 5) Your timeline, and 6) Budget range if known.

Do you serve clients outside Delhi NCR?

Absolutely! We serve clients across India through remote collaboration. We've successfully delivered projects for clients in Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, and many other cities.

What are your payment terms?

We require 50% advance to start work, 40% upon design approval, and 10% upon project completion. We accept UPI, bank transfer, and online payments.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes! All our packages include at least 1 year of free support. We also offer maintenance plans for ongoing support, updates, and security monitoring.
